CONFERENCE REGISTRATION


To register: use the registration form and send it back to the mail address of the Conference.
vextal@byron.cgm.unive.it

You are kindly requested to bring a copy of this form together with a copy of the bank transfer documents to the conference secretariat at your arrival.
To register early for the conference (see fees) your registration form and payment reach us by 15 July 1999.
The conference registration fee includes: conference fee, conference package, welcoming party (Sunday), reception, 6 coffee breaks, and Proceedings.

Normal Fee Lit. 300.000 $ 160 Euro 150
Student Fee Lit. 100.000 $ 60 Euro 50
