Social events

- Venetian Baroque Chamber Music Concert - Monday night, September 22nd

- Social Dinner - Tuesday night, September 23rd

All workshop participants interested in either events should send a message to the local organizer. Payments can be made at the registration desk.
Tickets to the concert are 19 euros in case we manage to reach the quota for groups, i.e. 15 people. Otherwise they are 25 euros per person.
Cost of the banquet is 35 euros per person. The restaurant is one of the most typical Venetian "trattoria"s and the food is guaranteed delicious.

Local organizer contact: delmont@unive.it
