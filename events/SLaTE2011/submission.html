Paper submission

You are cordially invited to submit papers for the SLaTE-2011 workshop.
Deadline for the submission of 4-p papers: April 18, 2011.

Full 4-page papers should be submitted before April 18, 2011 as PDF attachments to an email with subject 'SLaTE-2011 submission' to SLaTE2011-paper (at) let.ru.nl. Notification of acceptance or rejection of papers will be given by June 10, 2011. All papers must be formatted according to the L2WS 2010 paper kit (download the kit here). If you have questions, you can send an e-mail to SLaTE2011-org [at] let.ru.nl.
