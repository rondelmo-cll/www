PREDICATE ARGUMENT STRUCTUREs

  • When building F-structures we incorporate Nuclear Modifiers, i.e. di/daPPs in Italian (of/by) into close complements functions;

  • We compute subcategorization dependancies for Verbs, Adjectives and deverbal/deadjectival nominal heads;

  • All remaining NPs and PPs are treated as ADJs or simply dangling NPs and are ignored unless they contain spatiotemporal locations.
